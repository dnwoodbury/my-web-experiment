Biography

Innovating Academic Libraries

I serve as the Department Head for Learning Spaces & Services at the North Carolina State University Libraries. In this role, I manage technology-rich learning environments at both the D.H. Hill Jr. and James B. Hunt Jr. Libraries.

My work focuses on the intersection of physical space and emerging technology. I oversee makerspaces, digital media labs, virtual reality exploration rooms, and collaborative computing areas. My goal is to help lead innovative teaching and learning programs that support students and faculty in the digital age.

Previously, I worked in the Higher Education Division of John Wiley & Sons Publishers. I hold a Master of Science in Information Science from UNC-Chapel Hill and a B.A. in English from the University of Kansas.
